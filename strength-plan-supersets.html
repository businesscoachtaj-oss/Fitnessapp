Trainer note: Run A then B back-to-back with no rest between. Then 45 sec rest while she does the mobility move. Lower and upper don't compete for blood flow — so she can actually push both. Three rounds of each superset, then move on.
Warm-Up
4 min
Bodyweight Squat
3 sec down, pause at bottom. Wakes up hips and knees.

Glute Bridge Hold
2 sec hold at top each rep. Activates glutes, opens hip flexors.
1 × 12
Band Pull-Apart
shoulderLight band, arms straight. Warms the rear delt and rotator cuff before any pressing or pulling.
1 × 15
Superset 1 — Squat + Row
9 min
SS1 Do A then B with no rest between 3 rounds
A
Goblet Squat
shoulderDB at chest. Deep as she can, tall spine. Push the weight — this is her best loading opportunity.
10–12 reps
B
DB Bent-Over Row
Both arms or single braced. Elbow close to body, full pull. Upper back does the work — shoulder is safe here.
10–12 reps
🌀 Rest 45 sec: 90/90 Hip Switch — sit on the floor, rotate hips side to side × 8 slow reps. "Just catching your breath."
Superset 2 — Hinge + Press
9 min
SS2 Do A then B with no rest between 3 rounds
A
Romanian Deadlift (DBs)
3 sec descent. Load the hamstrings. She knows this — push the weight, demand the tempo.
10 reps
B
Chest-Supported Incline DB Press
shoulderLow incline bench, face down. Press forward at 30–45°. No impingement risk in this position. Moderate weight, controlled.
10–12 reps
🌀 Rest 45 sec: Low lunge hip flexor stretch — step one foot forward, back knee down, tuck the pelvis. 20 sec each side. She's just "resting between sets."
Superset 3 — Lunge + Shoulder
6 min
SS3 Do A then B with no rest between 2–3 rounds
A
Reverse Lunge (DBs)
Step back, front shin stays vertical. More hip range and easier on knees than a forward lunge. Single-leg work she can handle.
8 each side
B
Prone Y-T Raises
shoulderFace down on incline bench. Y = arms at 45° thumbs up. T = arms straight out. Bodyweight or very light. Builds rotator cuff health — worth doing every session.
10 reps
🌀 Rest 45 sec: Figure-4 stretch on the bench — cross ankle over knee, sit back slightly. 20 sec each side. Zero setup, barely looks like stretching.
Core Finisher
2 min
Dead Bug
Back flat, opposite arm and leg extend slowly. Strong anti-extension work with zero shoulder load.
2 × 8 each
Pallof Press (band or cable)
shoulderPress from chest, hold 2 sec, return. Arm stays at or below shoulder height. Anti-rotation finisher — she'll feel it.
2 × 10 each
