◆ How These Standards Are Calculated
Standards are based on bodyweight multipliers derived from NSCA guidelines, Kilgore & Rippetoe strength standards, and ExRx performance data. Values shown as 1RM (one rep max) by default — toggle to 5RM to compare working weights (Epley formula: 5RM ≈ 1RM × 0.867). Pull-ups and chin-ups are always in reps at bodyweight. Carries are total load for 30 metres. Dumbbell exercises show per-dumbbell load. Age adjustment applies from 35+.
