How CT Fitness Works

No fads. No guesswork. Every recommendation is backed by current sports science and tailored to the individual.

01
Evidence-Based Programming
Programs are built on validated research — volume landmarks from RP, progressive overload principles, and periodisation models that have stood up to scientific scrutiny. No bro-science.
02
Individual-First Approach
Your schedule, recovery capacity, injury history and goals dictate the program — not the other way around. Cookie-cutter plans don't get lasting results.
03
Long-Term Retention
The goal isn't just results — it's keeping them. Clients stay because the methods are sustainable, the education is real, and training becomes a habit rather than a chore.
04
Nutrition as a Tool
Nutrition guidance is grounded in science — not restriction. Understanding your numbers (TDEE, protein, macros) is empowering, not complicated. The tools on this site make that accessible for everyone.
