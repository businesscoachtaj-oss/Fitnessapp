How Programs Work

Built differently.

01
Periodised, not random
Every program has a clear structure — volume builds week by week toward a planned peak, then a structured deload. This is how muscle is actually built, not by doing the same thing every week and hoping.
02
Substitutes for everything
Every exercise has 2–3 alternatives built in. If you don't have a cable machine, a specific bar, or a particular piece of kit — you're not stuck. The program adapts to your gym, not the other way around.
03
Loads into your planner
Every program connects directly to the CT Fitness Workout Planner. One tap and your week is pre-filled — exercises, sets, rep targets. You just show up and train.
04
Spreadsheet included
Every program comes with a full 8-week tracking spreadsheet — dark-themed, day-by-day, with columns for weight, reps, RIR and notes. Track on paper or on your phone — the data is yours.
