◆ Frequently Asked Questions
How does weekly billing work? +
Your card is automatically charged each week on the same day you signed up. Payments are processed securely through Stripe — CT Fitness never stores your card details. You can cancel at any time from your member dashboard and your access continues until the end of the billing period.
Can I pause my subscription? +
Yes — if you're travelling, injured, or just need a break, you can pause your subscription from your dashboard. Pausing stops billing and resumes when you're ready. Just give Taj a heads-up so he can adjust your program on return.
What happens after I sign up? +
Once payment is confirmed, Taj will reach out within 24 hours to complete an onboarding form covering your goals, training history, schedule and any injuries. Your first program is usually delivered within 48 hours.
Is online coaching as effective as in-person? +
For most people, yes — especially if you already have some training experience. The program, guidance and accountability are identical. The main difference is real-time form correction, which is why in-person PT is recommended for complete beginners or those with complex movement issues.
Do I need a gym membership? +
For online coaching, programs can be written for any environment — commercial gym, home gym, or minimal equipment. For in-person PT, sessions are at Crows Nest (455 Pacific Highway) which has full equipment access.
Can I switch between plans? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade from online coaching to in-person PT or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Just message Taj or update from your member dashboard.
