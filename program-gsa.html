Program Overview

Built for Results

Goal
Aesthetic Development
Glute growth, shoulder width, core definition — built to turn heads
Training Style
Hypertrophy + Sculpt
Volume-based training with progressive overload and metabolic finishers
Split Structure
Push / Pull / Lower × 2
Double frequency for priority muscles — glutes, delts and abs hit every session
Equipment
Full Gym Required
Barbell, dumbbells, cables, hip thrust machine or bench, resistance bands
Rest Day
Day 7 (Sunday)
Active recovery recommended — walking, stretching, light mobility work

01
Glutes
The centrepiece of this program. Every lower day is structured around maximal glute activation — hip thrusts, RDLs, cable kickbacks and more. Expect serious growth over 8 weeks.
Glute Max Glute Med Hip Flexors Hamstrings
02
Shoulders
Width creates the illusion of a smaller waist. This program hammers all three heads — front, side and rear — through presses, lateral raises, cables and face pulls.
Front Delt Side Delt Rear Delt Traps
03
Abs & Core
Abs are trained 4x per week with a mix of weighted and bodyweight movements — from cable crunches and hanging leg raises to Pallof presses for deep core stability.
Rectus Abdominis Obliques TVA Serratus
