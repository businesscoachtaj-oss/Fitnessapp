Program Philosophy

How This Program Works

🎯
Goal-Oriented Periodisation
Every exercise, every set, every rep is chosen because it directly moves the needle on one of the 9 strength standards. There is no filler. If an exercise isn't closing the gap between your current level and Elite, it isn't in this program. The standards are the program.
📈
4-Block Linear Periodisation
The program progresses through four distinct phases — Hypertrophy Base, Strength Build, Peaking, and Max Test — each building on the last. Rep ranges drop and load increases every 4 weeks. This is the most evidence-backed approach to building a new 1RM from any starting point.
⚡
Test Weeks Every 4 Weeks
Week 4, 8, 12 and 16 are test weeks. You perform AMRAP (as many reps as possible) sets on your working weight, then use the Epley formula to estimate your new 1RM. This gives you objective data on progress — and maps you to your current standard level in real time.
⚖️
Lagging Lift Priority
Enter your current lifts in the tracker below. The program is designed so that your weakest lifts relative to the standards always get trained first — heaviest, freshest, most CNS drive. Your strongest lifts are maintained, not driven further ahead. This produces the fastest path to an overall Elite rating.

The Program

4 Blocks · 4 Days / Week

Upper Heavy and Lower Heavy sessions are your strength days — compounds first, progressive overload every session, full rest. Volume days accumulate hypertrophy. Every 4th week is a test week — you earn your numbers, then you record them.
